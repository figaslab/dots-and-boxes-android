🔲 DOTS AND BOXES RULES

Classic Dots and Boxes rules on a grid of dots:
• Players take turns drawing a single horizontal or vertical line between two adjacent dots
• When a player completes the fourth side of a box, they claim that box and score a point
• Completing a box earns an extra turn
• The game ends when all lines have been drawn
• The player who claimed the most boxes wins
• Chain strategy: force your opponent to give you multiple boxes in sequence
• Simple to learn, with surprisingly deep strategic depth

Whether you're a beginner connecting your first dots or a strategic thinker mastering chain sacrifices and double-dealing, this app provides the perfect Dots and Boxes experience both online and offline.
